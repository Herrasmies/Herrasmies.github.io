Selected publications

To date (11.1.2023), I’ve (co)authored 55 scientific papers, 46 of which have been peer-reviewed (18 as first author, 8 as second author, and 9 as last author). My work is multidisciplinary, and I have published papers focusing, in order of personal importance, on 1) gambling, games, and behavioral addictions, 2) decision-making, and psychophysiology of emotions and memory, 3) social psychology and ethics of artificial intelligence, and 4) psychology of deception.

Below is a snapshot of my research (OA = open access). For a more comprehensive list, see here. If you want a paper you can’t access, I’ll be happy to email you a copy.
